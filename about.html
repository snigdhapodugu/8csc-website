Our Mission

The Eighth College Student Council (8CSC) serves as the official representative body for all students within Eighth College at UC San Diego. We are committed to fostering a supportive community that enhances the student experience through advocacy, programming, and student engagement.

Our mission is to:

  • Represent student interests and concerns to college administration
  • Organize events and initiatives that build community
  • Provide funding opportunities for student organizations
  • Facilitate communication between students and college leadership
  • Promote pro-Blackness and anti-racism within our community

Our History

Eighth College, UC San Diegoâ€™s newest college, was established in 2023. That same year, the Eighth College Advisory Board was formed to lay the foundation for student governance and to guide the creation of the Eighth College Student Council, which was officially established in 2024.

Since then, we have worked to strengthen student representation and build community. Some of our key milestones include:

  • Establishing the Eighth College Student Activity Fee Referendum
  • Advocating for the Triton U-PASS Fee Referendum
  • Developing partnerships with other college councils across campus
  • Advocating for improved student services and resources
