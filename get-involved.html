General Body Meetings (GBMs)

Meeting Details

All Eighth College students are welcome to attend our weekly meetings.

  • Where: Podemos 276

What to Expect

  • Updates on council initiatives and events
  • Funding request presentations and votes
  • Open discussion on student concerns
  • Committee reports and announcements
